MovieStreamer


Leveraging the excellent Mongoose Web Server and HTML5 technology to stream movies from an apple computer to HTML5 compatible browsers on windows, mac, linux, and mobile devices on the local network. Store your personal movies on your mac and watch them on any device with a supported browser in your household.
